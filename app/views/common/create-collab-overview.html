This is your Neurorobotics Collab

Congratulations, you just created your own Neurorobotics "Collab", the collaborative platform where you can share knowledge and experiments.

Customize your Collab

This is your Collab default Overview page. You can customize it by clicking the configuration wheel at the top of the page.

Use the pages and left menu configuration wheel to modify the page's content and edit/delete menu items.

Create an experiment

The Neurorobotics platform offers a set of pre-defined experiments than can be copied and customized to your needs.

Navigate to My experiment to create your first copy of an existing experiment.

To add further experiments, click the ADD button in the left menu and choose the Neurorobotics App.

Launch your experiment

Launching your experiment will run the simulation and take you to the 3D visualization page where you can interact with the simulation.

Customizations done during the simulation are persisted in your Collab storage.

Share your experiments

Invite your team members into this Collab and share your experiments. You can communicate using the Collaboration chat on the right and share files using the Collab storage.

Navigate to the Team page to invite a team member.

Collab storage

The Collab storage is the space where all the files related to your experiments are stored.

Navigate to the Storage page to manage your files.

Find out more

For more information on how to run and customize your experiments and manage your Collab refer to the Neurorobotics Collab support page or have a look at the Neurorobotics platform user manual.
